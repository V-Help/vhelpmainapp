About VHELPCC

Your All-in-One Campus Solution

VHELPCC is a comprehensive platform designed specifically for VIT Chennai students and staff. Our app streamlines campus services by bringing together repair services, food delivery, lost & found functionality, and much more in one convenient location.

Built with modern technology and user experience in mind, VHELPCC makes campus life easier through intuitive design, real-time updates, and secure authentication. Whether you need to fix your laptop, order food, find a lost item, access study materials, join clubs, or buy used goods, we've got you covered.

  • Seamless user experience across all devices
  • Real-time tracking and updates
  • Secure JWT-based authentication
  • Multi-role access control system

Core Features

Repair Services

Submit hardware or facility repair requests directly via the app. Track progress and receive notifications when your issue is resolved.

  • Quick request submission
  • View repair history
  • Automatic progress updates
  • Photo documentation support

Food Delivery

Browse local campus eateries, order food, and get real-time order tracking until your meal arrives at your location.

  • Restaurant selection & menus
  • Secure online payments
  • Live order tracking
  • Delivery time estimates

Lost & Found

Report lost items, search reported finds, and manage claims securely and efficiently with photo verification.

  • Easy reporting of lost/found items
  • Photo uploads for verification
  • Admin moderation system
  • Secure claim process
