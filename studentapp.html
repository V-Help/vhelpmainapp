Revolutionizing VIT Chennai Campus Life

Your Digital Campus Companion

VHELPCC is a comprehensive Flutter-based mobile application that transforms how VIT Chennai students interact with campus services. Built with cutting-edge technology and designed for 15,000+ students, our platform integrates food ordering, repair services, lost & found, study materials, clubs, and marketplace features into one seamless experience.

From ordering food from multiple campus restaurants to finding lost items, accessing study materials, joining clubs, and buying used goods - VHELPCC is your one-stop solution for everything campus-related. Built by students, for students.

  • Multi-restaurant food ordering with real-time tracking
  • Comprehensive repair services for devices and facilities
  • Smart lost & found system with photo verification
  • Organized study materials and academic resources
  • Campus clubs and organizations directory
  • Peer-to-peer marketplace for used goods
  • Faculty information and communication system
  • Secure authentication with VIT email validation

Core Campus Services

🍕 Food Delivery System

Order from multiple campus restaurants with real-time menu updates, smart cart management, and live order tracking. Rate and review restaurants to help the community make better choices.

  • Multi-restaurant platform with live menus
  • Real-time order tracking and notifications
  • Smart cart with automatic restoration
  • Community ratings and reviews
  • Peak hour delivery optimization

🔧 Device & Facility Repairs

Submit repair requests for mobiles, laptops, and campus facilities. Track progress in real-time with photo documentation and direct communication with service providers.

  • Mobile and laptop repair services
  • Campus facility maintenance requests
  • Real-time status tracking
  • Photo documentation support
  • Service provider communication

🔍 Lost & Found Hub

Advanced lost and found system with photo verification, location-based search, and admin moderation. Connect with fellow students to recover lost items safely.

  • Photo upload for item verification
  • Location-based search functionality
  • Admin moderation for authenticity
  • Secure claim process
  • Community-driven recovery system
